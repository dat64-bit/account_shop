# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Admin**: Shop owners and staff managing inventory, orders, plans, and customer tickets.
- **Customers**: End-users who browse, purchase digital accounts, manage their subscriptions, and seek support.

## Product Purpose
To provide a robust, premium e-commerce and subscription management platform for digital accounts. It allows seamless purchasing (both instant and on-demand delivery) and efficient administrative management of the storefront.

## Brand Personality
Premium, Professional, Minimalist. The interface should feel high-quality, trustworthy, and clean.

## Anti-references
- Do NOT use playful, childish fonts or messy layouts.
- Avoid heavy, thick shadows or highly saturated, colorful gradients.
- Avoid clunky, dense, or cluttered Admin interfaces; don't cram too many buttons into small spaces without proper hierarchy.

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity over Decoration**: Every element must serve a functional purpose. Let the data and actions breathe with generous spacing.
2. **Premium Precision**: Use subtle micro-animations and crisp typography to convey high quality rather than loud colors.
3. **Task-Focused Workspaces**: Keep users in context by using local state transitions (e.g., inline forms and details) rather than overwhelming them with modals or full-page reloads.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG AA Compliance**: Must support proper semantic HTML for screen readers.
- Fully navigable via keyboard (logical tab order, clear focus indicators).
- Maintain minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratios for body text.
